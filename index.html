Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my process, services, and how we can work together.

My process is collaborative and iterative, centered on a deep understanding of your business goals and user needs. It typically involves four phases: 1. Discovery & Strategy (workshops, research, brief), 2. Design & Prototyping (wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, interactive prototypes), 3. Development Handoff (style guides, assets), and 4. Support & Iteration.

I offer flexible pricing models to suit your needs. For well-defined projects, I provide a fixed-price quote. For projects where the scope may evolve, I work on a daily or weekly rate. I'm always transparent about costs upfront and will provide a detailed proposal before any work begins.

I am typically booked 2-4 weeks in advance but can sometimes accommodate more urgent projects. The best way to find out is to get in touch. Please reach out via the contact form with details about your project, and I'll get back to you with my availability.

For personal projects and portfolios like this one, I often use GitHub Pages. It's a free, fast, and incredibly reliable way to host static websites directly from a code repository. It not only simplifies the deployment process but also showcases my workflow with Git and version control. While I manage custom domains for client projects, GitHub Pages is my go-to for development and personal showcases.
